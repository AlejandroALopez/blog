About me

Greetings! My name is Alejandro Lopez. I lived in Lima, Peru for most of my life and graduated from Dartmouth College. I am a US resident now and I am starting my career as a Software Engineer.

One of the accomplishments I am most proud of is being a member of the Digital Applied Learning and Innovation Lab (DALI) during my 4 years at Dartmouth. I was able to meet wonderful people and work on lots of meaningful projects (some of which have articles on this blog).

My personal interests include learning new dev tools by building projects, UI/UX design on Figma, and improving my efficiency at doing all kinds of tasks (e.g. I am a fan of Pomodoro timers). Some of my hobbies are cooking (Peruvian dishes mostly), playing videogames, archery, and boxing.
